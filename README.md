# 🛒 Online Sales E-Commerce: Performance & Insights Dashboard

Welcome to the **Online Sales E-Commerce** repository! This project delivers an end-to-end analytical suite designed to convert raw transactional data into actionable business intelligence, focusing on sales growth, profitability, and operational efficiency.

[📊 View Project Repository](https://github.com/mayurlokmanwar-analyst/Online-Sales-E-Commerce-Dashboard)

---

## 🚀 Overview
This dashboard acts as a central nervous system for e-commerce decision-makers. By bridging the gap between raw data and visual storytelling, it enables stakeholders to track financial health, understand customer baskets, and evaluate the efficiency of the entire supply chain.

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):
- **Total Revenue & Profit:** Consolidated view of financial health and margins.
- **Order Volume & Quantity:** Monitoring market demand and sales velocity.
- **Average Order Value (AOV):** Analyzing customer purchasing power and basket size.
- **Shipping Performance:** Identifying operational bottlenecks in the delivery cycle.

- **Data Engineering:** Used **Power Query** to handle duplicates and missing values, while engineering new features like **Shipping Duration** and **Profit Segments**.
- **Data Modeling:** Built an optimized **Star Schema** (Fact Sales connected to Customer, Product, Date, and Region dimensions) for seamless cross-filtering.
- **Advanced DAX:** Created measures for **YoY Growth**, **Monthly Trends**, and dynamic **Category-wise Contribution** analysis.

## 💡 Business Insights & Impact
* **Regional Intelligence:** Identified high-revenue territories and pinpointed underperforming markets for targeted marketing spend.
* **Profitability vs. Volume:** Revealed which categories drive massive revenue versus those that contribute the highest net profit margins.
* **Logistics Optimization:** Exposed specific shipping delays, providing the data needed to streamline carrier partnerships and warehouse operations.
* **Customer Loyalty:** Highlighted repeat-purchase patterns and shifts in Average Order Value over time.
