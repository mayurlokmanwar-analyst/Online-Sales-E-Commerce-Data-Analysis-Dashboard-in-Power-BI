## 🚀 Strategic Overview
This dashboard serves as the central nervous system for e-commerce decision-making. By bridging the gap between raw transactional data and visual storytelling, this suite enables stakeholders to monitor financial health, optimize customer baskets (AOV), and evaluate the efficiency of the end-to-end supply chain.

### 📈 Core Business KPIs
| **Metric** | **Value Focus** | **Business Impact** |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Revenue & Profit** | Net Margin Tracking | Real-time visibility into bottom-line health. |
| **AOV** | Average Order Value | Analyzing purchasing power and upsell potential. |
| **Sales Velocity** | Order & Quantity Volume | Monitoring market demand and seasonal spikes. |
| **Logistics** | Shipping Performance | Identifying bottlenecks in the fulfillment cycle. |

## 💡 Strategic Business Insights
* **Territorial Intelligence:** Identified top-performing regions and pinpointed underperforming markets to optimize marketing ROI.
* **Margin Optimization:** Discovered the delta between high-volume categories vs. high-profit categories to guide inventory investment.
* **Supply Chain Efficiency:** Exposed specific carrier delays, providing data-backed evidence to renegotiate logistics partnerships.
* **Customer Retention:** Highlighted repeat-purchase patterns and identified shifts in purchasing behavior across different time horizons.
